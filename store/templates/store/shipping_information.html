Shipping Information

Available Shipping Methods

At Orbital Bookstore, we offer multiple shipping options to meet your needs. Whether you need your books delivered quickly or prefer a more cost-effective option, we've got you covered!

Shipping Options

  • Standard Shipping - Estimated delivery time: 5-7 business days. Flat rate: $4.99.
  • Express Shipping - Estimated delivery time: 2-3 business days. Flat rate: $9.99.
  • Overnight Shipping - Next-day delivery for orders placed before 12 PM. Flat rate: $19.99.
  • International Shipping - Estimated delivery time: 7-14 business days. Rates vary based on location.

Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-3 business days (excluding weekends and holidays) after receiving your order confirmation email. You will receive another notification when your order has shipped.

Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, we will send you a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this tracking number to follow your package as it makes its way to you.

Shipping Restrictions

Please note that we currently do not ship to PO Boxes. Additionally, some countries may have restrictions or additional customs fees which are not included in our shipping rates.

Free Shipping

Enjoy free standard shipping on orders over $50 (US only). This offer is automatically applied at checkout when your order exceeds the qualifying amount.

Returns and Exchanges

If you are not fully satisfied with your purchase, we accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Please contact us for more details.
